Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Expenseia (the “App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1) Overview

Expenseia helps you track expenses and insights. The App is provided by Omkar Mandave. This policy applies to all users of the App and related services.

2) Information We Collect

  • Expense Data: Transactions you enter manually and/or parsed from permitted sources (e.g., transactional SMS).
  • Device Info: Basic device data (model, OS version) for diagnostics and crash reports (if crash/analytics SDKs are enabled).
  • Support Communications: Messages you send to our support email or forms.

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve core App features (expense tracking, categories, insights).
  • Parse eligible transactional SMS into expenses (if you grant SMS permission).
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, changes, or support.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4) App Permissions

Expenseia requests only the permissions needed for its functionality. You can control permissions in your device settings at any time.

Permission Purpose Is it Required?
SMS (READ_SMS / RECEIVE_SMS) Detect transactional bank/wallet/UPI messages to automatically add expenses. We ignore personal or OTP content and process data on-device. Optional. App works without SMS by manual entry.

You can revoke permissions from Settings → Apps → Expenseia → Permissions. Some features may be limited after revocation.

5) Data Storage & Retention

  • On‑device first: Expense data and parsed SMS insights are stored locally on your device.
  • Retention: We retain data for as long as you use the App or until you delete it from within the App or request deletion.
  • Backups: No Backups if you the delete app then you lose app data

6) Sharing & Transfers

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • If data is processed outside your country, we rely on appropriate safeguards where required by law.

7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information (store locally on device). No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data continuously.

8) Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: View and edit your expenses within the App.
  • Deletion: Delete entries in the App or request account/data deletion via email.
  • Permissions Control: Grant or revoke SMS/Notification permissions anytime.
  • Opt‑out of Analytics: If analytics are included, you can disable them from Settings (if provided) or contact us.
  • India (DPDP Act 2023): You may have rights to access, correct, and erase personal data and to grievance redressal. Contact us to exercise these.

To submit a request, email us at omandave2003@gmail.com. We may verify your identity before acting on requests.

9) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the age defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us to delete it.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and update the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes may be notified in‑app.
